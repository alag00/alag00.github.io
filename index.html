About

My name is Alexander Agfemalm. I am a game developer and programmer that aspires to work for a game company.

I have always been fascinated by the magic that is behind the creation of games. As long as I can remember, I have always been playing games. It started with GameBoy Advanced titles like Pokemon Sapphire or Kirby and the amazing mirror. My main source of games quickly shifted towards Playstation 2 where I played games from all sorts of genres, Fightin, Sports, Platformers and hack n slash games. The most memorable game for me on the PS2 is the Crash Bandicoot game Twinsanity. My enjoyment of video games exploded when I jumped to PC games, with huge titles like Minecraft, Team Fortress 2 and League of Legends, games that still are played daily by many people. I believe the reason was because these games were multiplayer which offered a huge amount of replayability.

I spent 3 years studying Game Design at Uppsala University to learn how to structurally construct a video game from a gameplay designer and programmer view. During my education I was taught how to create games using Unity, Unreal Engine and even without an engine by using an API for rendering graphics like OpenGL. I created a few short games through group projects for some of the university courses. I spent my exam thesis on researching the audio aspect of the horror game Darkwood. Currently I am searching for a job in this field while improving my skills by developing games.
